About The Forge

Some extra information

For over 10 years, The Forge has been RPI's hands-on home for makers, builders, and creators. What started as a small space for curious students has grown into one of the most active makerspaces on campus, powered entirely by the people who use it.

Our printers have run so long and so hard that many of them have crossed the 1,000-day mark of total print time, a testament to how much our members build and create. One of our machines has printed so many parts that the length of printed material can go around the world.
